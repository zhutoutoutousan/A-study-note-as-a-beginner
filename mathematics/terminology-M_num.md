## Prime numbers
- Definition
  - A number $p$ is said to be *prime* if $p > 1$ and $p$ has no positive divisors except $1$ and $p$.
- Theorem
  - Theorem 1: 正整数除1之外，皆为质数之乘积
    - Claim: *Every positive integer, except $1$, is a product of primes.*
