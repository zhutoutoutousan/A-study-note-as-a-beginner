# Definitions and notations
## Vector
### Vector.definition
A vector is an ordered finite list of numbers.
$$
\left[
\begin{matrix}
        a \\
        b \\
        c \\
        d
\end{matrix}
\right]
$$

### Vector.attribute
- Elements(Entries, coefficinets, components): The values in the array.
- Size(dimension or length)
